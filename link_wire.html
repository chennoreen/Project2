Wirefram

The landing page simply composed by three parts. The brand and logo in the middle, The navigation bar below that contains the categories of the products, Some selections in the top right, including searching bar, account information, whishlist and shopping bag. By clicking on the makeup section on the nav bar, a yellow box that categories all types of cosmetic products will immediately pop up.

This is a wirefram of the product page. At the left hand side, you can find photos that shows the product, color, formula, and also how it looks on people with different skin colors. While on the right hand side, you can find all the information and description for the product and different colors of the lipstick. Also, you can click on the "add to car" botton or "add to whishlist"botton to furthur purchase the product.

If you still not sure whether the lipstick fit you or not, you can click on the yellow "Try On" botton to upload your photo.

After uploading, you will find that the your photo replace the product photos. By selecting the different colors, you can see how they goes on your face, which is pretty easy and convinient.
